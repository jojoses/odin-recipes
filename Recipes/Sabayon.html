Sabayon

A old and luxurious yet intricate way, to serve desserts or a sauce.

This recipe is for the lovers, a simple yet complicated and elegant way to impress the people around you.

Estimated time for this recipe is around 20 minutes, and serves four

The prerequistes for the Sabayon

  • 1 cup Champagne
  • 6 Large egg yolks
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  1. Insert whisk. Add 1 cup Champagne, 6 large egg yolks and 1 cup granulated sugar to the work bowl
  2. Secure the Cooking Lid with Steam Cap in place. The cooking time is set for 20 minutes at 160 degrees farenheit or 71 degrees celsius
  3. Sabayon should be thickened and light
  4. Serve with fresh berries
  5. Chef pro tip: Add 1/2 cup of heavy whip cream, fold whipped cream into the cooled Sabayon. for a more lighter,luxurious Sabayon
