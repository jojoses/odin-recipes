Tinto De Verano

A refreshing tasty spanish getaway.

This recipe is a great past-time when you are sitting on a beautiful sunny terrace.

Estimated time for this recipe is around 10 minutes or less, adjust the proportions based on amount of people

The prerequistes for the Tinto De Verano

  • Red Wine:preferably young,and fruity.Spansih garnacha or Pinot Noir.
  • Fanta lemon, if you cant find it use a mix of lemon and soda water. variations include orange soda, or simply sparkling water and simple syrup.
  • Citrus: Orange and Lemon slices.
  1. Grab a large pitcher and add the ice and bottled of red wine. then fill the rest with lemon soda, leaving a bit of space to add the citrus fruits.
  2. Slice up some oranges, lemons, or both and add to the pitcher. Give everything a slight stir, do not mix to much or you will lose the carbonation
  3. Serve Immedietaly!
  4. Chef pro tip: Add an ounce of Vermouth, totally optional but does taste great for those that like it
